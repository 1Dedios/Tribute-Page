Nikolas Tesla

The Genius Behind Our Modern World

Timeline of Nikolas Tesla's life:

  • 1859 - Tesla is Born
    It's believed Nikola Tesla was born at midnight. Lightning struck during a summer storm where he was born
    in Smiljan, Serbia. Thus, Tesla was nicknamed 'Child of the Storm.'
  • 1870 - Tesla's Death - almost
    Tesla went to Karlovac to complete High School. Shortly after his arrival back home in Smiljan, he
    contracted Cholera. He was hospitalized for a year, facing near-death.
  • 1875 - Tesla's College Experience
    Tesla enrolls in a Polytechnic school with a military scholarship. As you can imagine, Tesla was a high achieving student. Earning him a letter of commendation from the dean of the Technical Department. That didn't stop him from being involved on campus. Tesla started a cultural club.
  • 1880 - Tesla + Prague
    Tesla's uncle scrapes money together to help Tesla move to Prague to finish his studies. Lack of competence in Greek and Czech meant he could not enroll in the classes he wanted to take. As an alternative, he took classes in Philosophy. Unfortunately, he received no grades for the course.
  • 1881 - Tesla Officially Begins Role as Chief Electrical Engineer
    This is the year that Tesla moved to Budapest, Hungary to work for a telegraph company. He did not start his role immediately because the Budapest Telephone Exchange company was not in operation. Thus, he took a job as a draftsman in another company. After a couple of months, the BTS Company was opened and Tesla officially began. He introduced various performance improvements, during his time with BTS.
  • 1882 - Tesla + Edison
    Tesla switched jobs this time in Paris at the Continental Edison Company. This was Tesla's first crack at a new industry, installing indoor lighting resources. This is where Tesla's genius started to thrive. So much so, the management team promoted Tesla to the team producing dynamos and motors. Shortly after, Tesla moves to the U.S.
  • 1884 - Tesla's Own Team
    At this time Tesla starts a manufacturing division in New York. The needs of the department require Tesla to move to NYC permanently.
  • 1885 - Tesla's First Patents
    Tesla meets with attorney, Lemuel Serrel, to submit multiple patents. Serrel eases the introduction between Tesla and a group of businessmen. This meeting financed the light production company under Tesla's name. Here he developed an enhanced DC motor.
  • 1887 - Tesla + Electric motor
    Tesla invents an induction motor operated by alternating current. This technology is becoming ubiquitous in Europe and America. This invention offered several benefits. Specifically the motor was operated by a polyphase current, which creates a rotating magnetic field. This invention was officially patented in May 1988. This made motors more efficient because they no longer required the complex maintenance associated with replacing mechanical brushes.
  • 1890 - Tesla + Wireless Power Transmission
    This was the start of Tesla trying to come up with a wireless lighting system that was based on near field and capacitative coupling. He often would hold public demonstrations lighting up his Geissler Tubes.
  • 1891 - Tesla Coil Officially Patented
    At the age of 35, Tesla patents the famous Tesla Coil and becomes a naturalized citizen of the U.S.
  • 1943 - Tesla's Death
    Tesla dies in a New York City Hotel Room, at the age of 86. He died alone and his body was found by houskeeping. The cause of death was determined to be heart related. A couple of days after his death the FBI ascertained his belongings. John G Trump was the man evaluating Tesla's work, acquired by the FBI. Trump was an electrical engineer for the state of New York at the time.
