Timeline of Nikolas Tesla's life:

  • 1859 - Tesla is Born
    It's believed Nikola Tesla was born at midnight. Lightning struck during a summer storm where he was born
    in Smiljan, Serbia. Thus, Tesla was nicknamed 'Child of the Storm.'
  • 1870 - Tesla's Death - almost
    Tesla went to Karlovac to complete High School. Shortly after his arrival back home in Smiljan, he
    contracted Cholera. He was hospitalized for a year, facing near-death.
  • 1875 - Tesla's College Experience
    Tesla enrolls in a Polytechnic school with a military scholarship. As you can imagine, Tesla was a high achieving student. Earning him a letter of commendation from the dean of the Technical Department. That didn't stop him from being involved on campus. Tesla started a cultural club.
